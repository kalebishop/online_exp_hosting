Instructions

Hello!
In this survey you will be playing a cooperative cooking game.
You will play one of two chefs in a restaurant that serves onion soups.

Movement and interactions

You can move up, down, left, and right using the arrow keys, and interact with objects using the spacebar.

You can interact with objects by facing them and pressing spacebar. Here are some examples:

  • You can pick up onions by facing an onion dispenser or an onion on a counter and pressing spacebar.
  • If you are holding an onion or dish and are facing an empty counter, and press spacebar, you will put the onion/dish on the counter.
  • If you are holding an onion and are facing a pot that is not full, and press spacebar, you will put the onion in the pot.
  • If you are holding a dish and are facing a pot with a soup that has finished cooking, and press spacebar, you will move the soup from the pot to your dish.
  • If you are holding a dish with soup in it and are facing a serving area, and press spacebar, you will serve the soup.


NOTE: You and your teammate cannot occupy the same location at the same time. If both of you try to move into the same space, neither action will go through.



Cooking

Once 3 onions are in the pot, the soup will automatically start cooking. When a soup begins cooking, you'll notice a red number appear over the pot to denote the cook time. This number counts up to 20 at which point the soup is done. Each soup served (by either you or your teammate) awards 20 points to your team.



Goal

The goal of the game is for your team to maximize your points by delivering as many soups as possible within the provided time. Each round will last 80 seconds.
